Technical Delivery

We know it’s not always possible to predict when you’ll need an item delivered. When important goods are required urgently, immediate delivery is often the only way to solve the problem.

At Trans Global Express, our US same day delivery service offers you:

Simple Payment Options

Make a booking over the phone or via the website. 
If you are a new customer we can process your order and take payment via credit card over the phone. Account customers can make multiple bookings online and are then invoiced on a weekly or monthly basis.

Pick Up within 1 Hour

Through our partnerships with other national courier networks we have access to one of the largest fleet of delivery vehicles in the US. This means we are normally able to pick up your package within 1 hour of taking your booking.

Safe Transport of Your Goods

By using the correct equipment and techniques we can transport your goods safely. In addition, your order can be tracked online, and in case of emergencies we are able to contact our drivers by phone.

Free Goods in Transit Insurance

We include free insurance for up to £10,000 on all items delivered using our same day courier services.

Proof of Delivery

Our drivers are responsible for your goods until they have taken a signature to confirm a successful delivery. Proof of delivery can then be seen through the online tracking system or sent to you via email within 20mins of the recipient taking the goods.

Urgent Air Delivery Service

Do you require a long distance US delivery that needs to be delivered faster than by road? We can organise for your package to be delivered by air, collected at the airport and then driven straight to its destination.
